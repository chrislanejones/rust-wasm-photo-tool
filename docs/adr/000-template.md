true about the system today that makes the status quo untenable or a change

## Pre-mortem (mandatory)

It is six months from now and this decision caused an incident. What
happened? Walk the most likely failure. Then: what in the Decision above
mitigates it (kill switch, read-through fallback, staged rollout,
reversibility window)?

## Consequences

Follow-ups this creates. What the next person needs to know before touching
this area.
